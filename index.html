Reviews

5 Stars - after months of research
by Imre Horvath on June 24 2016

Can't believe the difference this makes compared to a 50inch Samsung for online multiplayer. I play Cod, Battlefield and Rocket League. The 1ms response is a dream and the 10-11ms input lag is nearly unmatched at any price range and is a must. The speakers lack bass but provide adequate sound gaming late at night. But given if you have this or plan to get it you will probably have a headset anyways. The stock rts1 mode is good given you set your console rgb to full. A tad sharp though. No real downside I could find to not give it 5 stars. Enjoy future buyers. Worth the money if you play online a lot, as I do.

5 Stars - Perfect
by Wil Hanbidge on Jan. 5 2015

This monitor is gorgeous! It looks really sleek and has really clear colours. The fast refresh rate is perfect for gaming or for videos. It's a nice big screen but I don't think I could use anything any bigger. The monitor is really simple to use so I set it up in the first 30 minutes that I had it with no hassle. It arrived very quickly and was well packaged so I was very impressed. I would definitely recommend this for anyone that wants a high end monitor that is better than almost anything even in higher price ranges. My only hit is that I wish it had 144hz.

4 Stars - Great Monitor. Not so great stand.
by Damon Ford on June 15 2015

Picture quality is fantastic. Love the Dual HDMI inputs, But the downside to this monitor is the stand, NO adjustability, I will be looking to invest in a VESA compatible stand for it (which at least its compatible with).
